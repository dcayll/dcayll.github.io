## Marinade

* 2 lbs skirt steak
* 1/2 cup water
* 1/3 cup fresh lime juice
* 1/2 tablespoon fajita seasoning
* 1/2 tablespoon garlic powder

## Directions

Marinade in fridge for 2 hrs then sous vide at ~135-140 F
