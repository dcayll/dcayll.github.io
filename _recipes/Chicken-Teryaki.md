Ingredients
For the Chicken: 
- 1.5 lbs (680g) boneless, skinless chicken thighs or breasts (cut into bite-sized pieces)
- 1 large onion (sliced)
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 cloves garlic (minced)
- 1-inch piece ginger (grated)
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- 3 tbsp honey
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar (or apple cider vinegar)
- 1 tbsp cornstarch + 2 tbsp water (slurry)
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- ½ tsp red pepper flakes (optional, for heat)
- 2 tbsp sesame seeds (for garnish)
- 2 green onions (chopped, for garnish)

For the Coconut Rice:
- 2 cups jasmine rice (rinsed)
- 1½ cups coconut milk (full-fat)
- 1¼ cups water
- ½ tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar (optional, enhances the coconut flavor)

Instructions

Make the Coconut Rice:
1. Rinse the jasmine rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
2. In a saucepan, combine rice, coconut milk, water, salt, and sugar.
3. Bring to a boil, then cover and reduce heat to low. Simmer for 15 minutes.
4. Remove from heat and let sit, covered, for another 5 minutes before fluffing with a fork.

Cook the Chicken:
1. Heat 1 tbsp oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add chicken and cook for 4-5 minutes per side until golden brown. Remove and set aside.
2. In the same pan, add another 1 tbsp oil and sauté the onions until soft and caramelized (about 5 minutes).
3. Add garlic and ginger, stir for 30 seconds until fragrant.
4. Pour in soy sauce, honey, rice vinegar, and red pepper flakes. Stir well.
5. Return the chicken to the pan and simmer for 3-4 minutes.
6. Mix cornstarch with water and add to the sauce, stirring until thickened (1-2 minutes).
7. Drizzle sesame oil over the chicken before serving.

Serve & Garnish:
- Plate the coconut rice, top with teriyaki chicken, and spoon extra sauce over it.
- Garnish with sesame seeds and chopped green onions.

Optional Add-ons:
- Serve with steamed broccoli, bok choy, or snap peas.
- Add a side of kimchi or pickled vegetables for extra flavor.
